### Problem In large or frequently edited drawings, balloons often become misaligned. Manually rotating balloon notes to match the drawing layout is repetitive and error-prone—especially when multiple balloons are involved. ### Solution This macro automatically orients drawing balloons **vertically or horizontally** based on the **leader direction (X/Y location)** and a user-selected orientation. It supports: - Single or **multiple balloon selections** - Consistent placement logic based on geometry - Quick execution directly from SolidWorks ### How It Works (High-Level) 1. User selects one or more balloons in a drawing 2. Macro prompts for desired orientation (vertical or horizontal) 3. For each selected balloon: - Evaluates leader endpoint X/Y direction - Determines optimal text rotation - Applies orientation while preserving leader attachment ### Demo *(Animated GIF showing before → macro execution → after)*
